Account Deletion Options

Please choose one of the following options to delete your account. Note: Only the in-app delete account feature (available from your Profile section when logged in) deletes your account and data immediately. Requests made through the Contact Us option or via the Google Form will be processed in due course.

Option 1: Request Deletion Without Logging In

If you are not logged in, please use the Contact Us option on the login screen to provide your account information and request account deletion.

Contact Us

Option 2: Delete Account Immediately (Logged In Users)

If you are logged in, go to your Profile section and click the Delete Account feature to remove your account and all associated data immediately.

Delete Account

Option 3: Submit a Deletion Request via Google Form

You can also visit our website to fill out a Google Form with your account information. This deletion request will be reviewed and processed, so it may take additional time.

Fill Out Deletion Request Form
